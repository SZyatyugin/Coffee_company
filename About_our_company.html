Large-Scale Wholesale Roasters for brand-name companies.

Our Company Group is called 40 Luxury Distributions, two Wholesale Roasters Coffee Companies located in Colombia and Spain, where we are two independent businesses yet joined in many aspects. We are Coffee Wholesalers, Exporters, Importers, Roasters and Coffee Bag Manufacturers.

Our Colombian Wholesale Coffee Company

In Colombia: We are a Colombian Coffee Wholesalers, Roasters and Exporters. We work directly with the farms. We control the quality of the coffee we supply in all its stages given that we are suppliers of the highest grades of coffee: 100% Arabica Excelso and Supremo Coffee, as also Organic Coffees from the most remote tribes in Colombia. We also deal with coffee beans from different regions of the planet. We supply coffee wholesale worldwide. As Wholesale Coffee Roasters and Suppliers in Colombia, we have managed, over the years, to obtain the best price possible for the highest-quality coffee by eliminating as many middle-men as possible. We are a Coffee Company that sells wholesale to 23 countries. The minimum orders from Colombia are 20-ft container.

Our Spain Wholesale Coffee Company

In Spain: We have 2 offices in Madrid and our factory is located in Alicante. Our Spain Coffee Company covers absolutely all aspecst in the coffee business. Our factory offers consists in latest up-to-date-machinery for large-scale roasting, bag manufacturing and packaging. Our team of external designers can help whichever clients who needs help designing their coffee bags.

We deal with coffees of all origins, though mainly with 6 of our company-selected beans and blends, ranging from Robustas to Premium Supremos. We supply coffee worldwide to traders, wholesalers, coffee shop, restaurant, hotel and supermarket chains. We specialize in Private Label, being one of the few Private Label Coffee Companies in the world to offer the best- price-value possible. Our structure allows us to provide the lowest possible prices to allow for profitable margins for our clients. We can provide practically any coffee from any origin the client requieres.

We can offer our clients a total and complete coffee service, from coffee bean, to coffee machinery to coffee bag on shelf! We also offer capsules and instant coffee as side products. All in one same place.
